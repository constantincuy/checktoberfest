💝 Our Commitment

Open Source at CHECK24

We believe in giving back to the community that powers our innovation.

240.000 € per year

Investment in Open Source

We dedicate substantial resources annually to support the open source projects that power our platform and help millions of users every day.

Our Mission

Over the last 20+ years, CHECK24 has built successful products on top of amazing open source projects. We're convinced that with financial success comes the obligation to give something back.

Since our products are based on open source, we have a huge self-interest to ensure that these projects are well-maintained and stable for the foreseeable future. In fact, our success depends on it.

"We are builders and want to support those who build for the community"

— Philipp Kemper, CTO CHECK24
